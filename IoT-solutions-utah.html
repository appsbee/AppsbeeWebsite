The world is moving beyond isolated devices. We are running into a new era where almost all the devices worldwide will be connected. The age of Internet ubiquity has arrived.

Google and Samsung are already ahead of the pack. Google bought smart thermostat maker and Nest Labs, last year for $3.2 billion, and Samsung purchased connected home company SmartThings for $200 million.
