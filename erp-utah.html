Managing Businesses In The Most Optimized Way With Our ERP Solutions

Modernized business units are so diversified that it often becomes challenging to manage all the resources and business processes manually. There is an ardent need to employ the most advanced technology and software in order to integrate the vital parts of the business seamlessly. Here comes the need for Enterprise Resource Planning(ERP).

Businesses will bring in more sales when customers are happy. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) ensures error-free data analysis using the right technology to drive in potential customers.

We Have A Huge Built-In ERP With Updated, New And Intuitive Functionalities For Branch Managers, Sales Representatives, Payments, Etc.

How Does Our ERP Solution Help?

  • Each employee, sales representative or branch manager will see the real-time data
  • Data may include the number of knocks on door-to-door sales, total serviceable and unserviceable sales, total amount earned in each account from sales, etc.
  • The employees can view canceled transactions

Our ERP Solutions With Mobility

  • The Branch manager will assign sales reps via pin location on Google Maps sales locations
  • Via the app the sales reps will know which doors to knock on

Recruitments and Rewards

  • Through ERP, it is decided which team will recruit a particular sales representative
  • There are tournament facilities, to award individuals and teams

Payments Through ERP

  • The employees will collect money from the customers through a payment gateway
  • The Payroll for the employees (includes taxes, employee’s room rent, etc.) is also managed through the ERP

Training Manual for Employees

  • We have a training manual for employees on door-to-door sales, modification of speech from branch managers, etc.

We Help Collect Donation for Local Charities

  • We enable customers to donate to the needy through our sales app
