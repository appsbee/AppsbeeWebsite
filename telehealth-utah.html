Telehealth Solutions

Remote Consultations Ensuring Smarter, Faster and Simpler Solutions for Patients

Our telehealth solution is easy to integrate and compatible with PC, mobile and tablet devices.

With the aging population and rising expenses, the healthcare industry is changing rapidly in the way it operates. Increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and lack of adequate medical expertise in the remote sectors are working as the catalysts for Telehealth’s rapid growth opportunities.

The graph of dependency on telemedicine is on a constant upward rise. The global telemedicine market size was approximately USD 18.20 billion in 2016 and is expected to reach nearly USD 64 billion by 2022, growing at a CAGR of around 14% between 2017 and 2022.

The Need for Telehealth

Remote Consultation

Most of the population lives in the countryside where there are no medical experts or specialists. For critical diseases, patients need to travel to the heart of the cities to receive their treatments. Here comes the demand for remote consultation.

Overseas Patients

Patients with critical diseases from underdeveloped countries often spend a huge amount of money to travel to the top-rated hospitals of the developed neighboring countries to get their treatments and follow-ups done. Telemedicine makes doctor-patient communication easy before and after travel.

Busy Schedules

People are so busy with their work, family and other obligations nowadays that they often find it hard to take time out of their schedules to go and visit a doctor. Thus, there is an ardent need for an instant healthcare service accessible at any time from anywhere.

Heavy Traffic

It might happen that we book a doctor’s appointment but fail to be on time due to heavy traffic or a prolonged meeting. We end up missing the appointment or are often left to pay the fees and get incomplete answers to our health issues due to late arrival. This is where telemedicine solutions can help.

Why it Benefits the Patients

Our superfast lifestyle in the era of digitization demands for instant diagnosis and treatment right from our PCs and smart-phones. Through telemedicine, patients can get doctor consultations from anywhere at any time, with just a few clicks.

How Patients benefit from our telehealth solution:

  • Search & schedule a doctor’s appointment from anywhere
  • Engage in a video consultation with a doctor in the morning, noon or night
  • Get accessibility of new prescriptions or adjusted doses
  • Request for lab test
  • Receive lab reports via email/app/website
  • Have a fast and easy way for second opinion
  • Receive estimated treatment costs
  • Make appointments for family members
  • Have medical records on a secured cloud(EMR)

Why it Benefits the Doctors and Hospitals

Through our telehealth solutions, healthcare organizations can seamlessly provide unparalleled services to the patients at the right hour, without delay.

  • Manage all appointments through an app called Appointment Manager
  • Real-time appointment notifications
  • Confirm appointments
  • Cancel appointments
  • Edit appointment date/time
  • Engage in video consultations with patients
  • Access patient’s medical history
  • Access patient’s health updates through hospital HIS (Our solution is very flexible and scalable and it should be connected to the HIS of the hospital to access all of the features)
  • Have a dedicated web page for every clinic/hospital

Why it Benefits the Medical Tourism Companies

With our telehealth solutions, medical tourism companies can add value to their services by establishing communication between patients - doctors - hospitals before and after their medical tour.

  • Implementation of video calls between providers and patients
  • HIPAA Texting
  • Real-time messaging
  • Group messaging
  • Mobile charge capture
  • Automated discharge, admission, transfer and clinical push notification
  • Rounding and call scheduling management
  • Case history
  • Secured patient portals

Technology

For video-based calling solution, we have extensively used WEBRTC based solutions like OpenTalk, Tokbox, etc. We have successfully connected mobile devices (both iOS and Android) and web applications via OpenTalk, which means, a call is initiated from an iPhone, the call gets through an Android device or a PC. We have built the rest of the implementation on LAMP stack.
